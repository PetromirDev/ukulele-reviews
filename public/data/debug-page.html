**Important notes about the ukulele reviews**

 I am often asked 'how the overall scores work'. The really simple way to consider them is...

Scores in 9s - absolutely essential and recommended by me
Scores in 8s - recommended by me - minor issues
Scores in 7s - reasonable, but some bigger issues you may need to be mindful of. Doesn't get a full recommendation but may suit you
Scores in 6s - as per 7s, but much bigger issues leading me towards suggesting you avoid unless it absolutely floats your boat and you can deal with setup issues
Scores 5s or below - avoid like the plague!

Bear in mind, all my reviews are COMPLETELY IMPARTIAL and subjective  and I don't do paid reviews, or good scores for free instruments (many sites do, not me). Subjective because they are one persons opinion and personal preference always comes into play (I do point out where views are subjective though and those that are objective.) And this is also not one of those dreaded 'uke reviews' sites which have just used Amazon reviews hoping you will click and earn them affiliate revenues-  these are real reviews! They are made up about 50% of ukuleles loaned to me by shops and brands with the other 50% bought by me with proceeds from the adverts on the site (it's the reason the site HAS to run adverts - to bring you more reviews). In either case I say so in the review. On rare occasions a brand (particularly with cheap instruments) decides its not cost effective to ship a ukulele back so they don't collect it.  These are either donated to local school, given to ukulele raffles for charity or on sold on to buy more ukes for review which, one way or another then end up going to charity.  Sorry to go on about that, but some people do like to claim that I work for  uke brands or a uke shops - I really DO NOT! In fact reviews that do that are utterly pointless.

I am in touch with all leading brands, so if a brand is missing from the list I will let you draw your own conclusions as to why! I suspect some are concerned with honesty..

Oh, and please - don't get too bothered if you disagree with a  review - these are just one man's subjective opinion (which is the ONLY way to review something.. I find it odd when people get annoyed by subjectivity..) If you have the same uke and love it, then that is cool too... naturally. In fact - this rant on the subject may interest you..
